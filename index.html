Hi there.

I'm Erin. I've been coding since 2011 and teaching myself Ruby on Rails and JavaScript since 2014. I use my master's degree in creative writing and my love of nostalgia and whimsy to build apps that are engaging and fun to use. With a background in copy editing and desktop publishing, I have an eye for detail, a love of clean styling, and passion for elegant products.

What's a full-stack Ruby on Rails developer?

It means I care about everything from user experience (a person's emotions and attitudes while using my products) to the robustness of my server-side test suites. Put more technically, it means I'm comfortable working in both the back end (server, application, and database) and the front end (parts of the web that you can see and interact with), and I know a bit about graphic design.
